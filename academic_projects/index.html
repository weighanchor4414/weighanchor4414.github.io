Autonomous Aerial Vehicle Challenge (AAVC) 2020

A high load capacity, long endurance Search and Rescue (SAR) quadrotor platform with folding arms to reduce footprint during transportation.
Has a maxmimum endurance of 18 minutes and can carry up to 5kg of payload with a max speed of up to 60km/h.
